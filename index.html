About Our Enterprise

Tanu Surgical Industries is a trusted name in the Indian healthcare sector, proudly serving hospitals, clinics, and medical institutions since 1972. Founded by the Late Shri Tilak Chand Jain, the company was built on a foundation of quality craftsmanship, integrity, and a commitment to advancing healthcare infrastructure.

Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Sanjay Kumar Jain, the organisation has grown from a domestic trading firm into a recognised manufacturer and exporter of premium surgical and hospital appliances, serving both domestic and international markets with well-established export capabilities.

"Every single item is forged using elite high-grade Stainless Steel (Grade 304), handpicked for extreme biological safety and uncompromising material longevity."
